The symbols of Freebia are many and Varied. These symbols include the national flag and the Freebian Seal. In addition, each province is represented by its own distinct flag. All images displayed here may be reproduced freely elsewhere provided they are accompanied by proper attribution and a link back.

The Freebian flag is one of the most unique flags in the world since not many nations have a flag with the colours of blue red & green together with a yellow star in the centre.the Freebian flag contains the green for the land. the red for Freebia's people. The blue is for the water in the ocean. as the yellow lines between the 3 layers has no official meaning, and the star means the president, Zakkary Graham.

The official seal of Freebia is a dark red circle with two flags & a star at the top which means the capital territory, and in the circle is Freebia's national Animal, The Magikarp. and next to the Magikarp is 4 stars, which mean the 4 other provinces. and above & below the Magikarp is the word "Freebia" and when it was founded.

The flag of Freebian Australia is the colour blue (which means the ocean surrounding Australia) with the coat of arms on a white square to the upleft corner, with a light yellow picture of Australia in the centre with the location of Freebian Australia with a red star.
The flag of The Pizza Penguin consists of The Flag of Italy, because it is located in the heart of Naples, Italy. and it has a Pizza on it since Pizza is primarily Italian. and it has the Freebian Coat of Arms in the Centre.
The flag of The Freebian African Territory consists of Tan at the top because it is mostly desert & tan is the colour of the sand. and in the middle is a map of the territory, at the bottom is blue because that land is landlocked and it needs water. and it has text called "We found the opprotunity" because it is between Egypt & Sudan and Neither country claims it, so Freebia finds the opprotunity to annex it as theirs.
The flag of The Freebian Capital Territory consists of the basic flag of Freebia, but with the coat of arms instead of a star & it has the text "Freebian Capital Territory" on it.
The flag of The West Goldfish consists of blue at the left for the Ocean & Water because it is near the ocean at Seattle Washington. at the centre is white with a raincloud & rain because it rains alot in that part of the world. and at the right is green which symbolizes the Forests & Trees.
