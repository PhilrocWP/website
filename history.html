The history of Freebia begins in Richmond Virginia on June 21st, 2014 when Zakkary Graham decided to create his own nation called "Circumspice" (A name choson for no particular Reason,) and created a flag for it, within a few weeks he had himself a website and declared himself president of Circumspice, but on July 18th, 2014 President Zakkary Graham decided to rename Circumspice to "Freebia" and with a new Website with it, and now we have the present as Freebia is relatively a relatively new state.

The timeline below does not have every thing that happens every day, just the major events that happens yearly. and we wish to emphasize you, dear viewer that this stuff is Real & that this stuff really happened. This is just a basic timeline of Freebia's history, not an exhaustive analysis.
